# TrueCombo Guide Authoring Playbook

Use this workflow for all current and future guide updates.

## 1) Research and grounding
- Collect high-signal context from reliable public sources (SmashWiki, competitive educational material, tournament/meta discussion).
- Focus on mechanics, decision points, common habits, and realistic counterplay.
- Do not copy wording or article structure from any source.

## 2) Coaching-first outline
- Intro: who this helps, what problem it solves in real matches.
- Core sections: practical situations (neutral, advantage, disadvantage, ledge, pressure).
- Mistakes section: what beginners commonly do wrong.
- Focus-first section: what to train first this week.
- Quick tips section: compact checks players can apply in-game.

## 3) Writing standard
- Be specific: describe spacing, burst range, timing, and risk/reward.
- Use adaptation language: "if they keep shielding", "if they panic jump", "if they drift in after hitstun".
- Add matchup-aware examples where helpful (Mario, Cloud, Joker, Samus, Kirby).
- Keep terminology natural: neutral, whiff punish, conditioning, ledgetrapping, DI, pressure, adaptation.

## 4) SEO without bloat
- Keep headings and metadata clear and search-intent aligned.
- Favor meaningful phrasing over repeated keywords.
- Preserve readable sentence flow and premium editorial tone.

## 5) Internal link checklist
- Link to at least one relevant mechanics guide.
- Link to at least one character or matchup guide when relevant.
- Link to glossary terms if jargon appears.

## 6) Publish QA
- Remove vague claims and filler.
- Remove unverifiable numbers/frame data unless explicitly sourced and needed.
- Confirm every section gives an actionable takeaway.
