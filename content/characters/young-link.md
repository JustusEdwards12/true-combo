## Character overview

Young Link is a fast projectile pressure character who converts light hits into long advantage sequences.

## Difficulty level

Intermediate

## Playstyle identity

Chip with arrows and boomerang, then burst in with fast aerials when shield and jump habits are exposed.

## Strengths

- Excellent projectile frame-to-frame pressure
- Strong combo routes from tilts and aerial starters
- Mobile enough to weave in and out of burst range

## Weaknesses

- Can struggle to close stocks without setup
- Gets punished hard if projectile spacing is sloppy
- Recovery can be sniped when predictable

## Best beginner gameplan

- Build percent through arrow and boomerang confirms
- Pressure shield safely with low-commit aerials
- Push opponents to ledge and force panic options

## Neutral strategy

- Use fire arrow to interrupt dashes and jumps
- Do not overcommit to boomerang at point-blank range
- Dash dance around burst range before committing nair

## Advantage state

- String up airs while tracking DI and platform routes
- At ledge, threaten z-drop and jump callouts
- Keep pressure with quick resets instead of greedy smashes

## Disadvantage state

- Use mobility first, buttons second
- Avoid directional airdodge habits toward center
- Challenge juggles only when anti-air lane is clear

## Recovery tips

- Mix tether and spin attack timing
- Recover from varied heights to avoid edgeguard scripts
- Hold jump until opponent commits to edgeguard

## Common beginner mistakes

- Throwing projectiles with no spacing behind them
- Overextending combo attempts and losing stage
- Using unsafe kill moves instead of ledge pressure

## Key moves

- Fire Arrow: quick neutral check
- Boomerang: confirms and corner control
- Nair: fast pressure tool
- Up air: juggle extender

## Out of shield options

- Nair out of shield at close range
- Up-B out of shield as hard punish
- Jump out of shield reset when spacing is safe

## Basic combos

- Down tilt into aerial chain at low percent
- Boomerang hit into forward air confirm
- Up throw into platform pressure routes

## Kill confirms

- Forward air callout near ledge
- Up-B out of shield punishes
- Projectile trap into smash read

## Matchup considerations

- Against swordies, prioritize drift and projectile timing
- Against rushdown, keep them in arrow check range
- Against heavies, farm combo damage and ledge traps

## Practical gameplay advice

- Young Link wins through layered tempo
- Do not panic fish kills when ahead
- Adapt projectile cadence between stocks

## Training priorities

- Projectile-to-aerial confirm drill
- Ledge trap option coverage reps
- Recovery route variation practice
