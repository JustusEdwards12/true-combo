## Character overview

Snake is a trap-control character who dominates space with grenades, explosives, and patient anti-approach play.

## Difficulty level

Intermediate

## Playstyle identity

Build explosive threat zones, force awkward movement, then punish panic entries with heavy damage.

## Strengths

- Exceptional stage control through explosives
- Strong punish damage from defensive openings
- Excellent ledge trapping with layered threats

## Weaknesses

- Can be overwhelmed in point-blank scrambles
- Projectiles require awareness to avoid self-punish
- Recovery can be targeted by prepared opponents

## Best beginner gameplan

- Set grenade tempo early
- Use anti-air and burst punishes for impatient approaches
- Control ledge with explosive layering

## Neutral strategy

- Keep grenade pull timings varied
- Do not autopilot explosive usage in close range
- Hold center and force opponents into hazard lanes

## Advantage state

- Trap ledge with grenade and movement coverage
- Juggle with up tilt threat and anti-air spacing
- Reset pressure when explosive cycle is broken

## Disadvantage state

- Use grenade trades to escape pressure
- Avoid panic directional airdodge to stage
- Mix landing routes to avoid anti-air scripts

## Recovery tips

- Vary cypher pathing and timing
- Recover around edgeguard positioning
- Use explosives to deter direct chases

## Common beginner mistakes

- Grenade pulling in unsafe scramble
- Predictable ledge explosive timing
- Overcommitting to high-risk reads

## Key moves

- Grenade: core control tool
- C4: stage denial and traps
- Up tilt: close kill punish
- Nair: scramble defense

## Out of shield options

- Grenade pull to break pressure rhythm
- Up smash/down smash trap response
- Shield grab when opponent lands too close

## Basic combos

- Grenade trade into positional advantage
- Nair hit into ledge pressure
- Explosive trap into follow-up punish

## Kill confirms

- Up tilt punishes at high percent
- C4 trap confirms
- Ledge explosive setups

## Matchup considerations

- Against rushdown, keep anti-approach discipline
- Against zoners, use explosive positioning over direct trades
- Against heavies, stack trap damage

## Practical gameplay advice

- Snake wins by forcing uncomfortable choices
- Your setup tempo should keep changing
- Take safe trades and hold center

## Training priorities

- Grenade timing reps
- Ledge trap explosive routes
- Recovery path variation
