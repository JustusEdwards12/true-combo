## Character overview

Pac-Man is a setup-heavy trap character who controls neutral through fruit management and hydrant pressure.

## Difficulty level

Intermediate

## Playstyle identity

Build item-based threat layers, force defensive decisions, then punish panic routes with prepared confirms.

## Strengths

- Exceptional trap and setup potential
- Flexible projectile toolkit
- Strong ledge pressure from prepared states

## Weaknesses

- Needs planning time and matchup knowledge
- Can be rushed if setup windows are denied
- Oversetup can surrender stage control

## Best beginner gameplan

- Charge fruit safely while controlling space
- Use hydrant to alter movement lanes
- Turn corner pressure into trap confirms

## Neutral strategy

- Mix fruit timings and choices to avoid predictability
- Do not overcommit to hydrant in close scrambles
- Hold center while preparing item states

## Advantage state

- Set ledge traps with fruit plus hydrant
- Catch landings with delayed projectile timing
- Use setups to force panic jumps

## Disadvantage state

- Avoid panic side-B from bad ranges
- Mix drift and defensive timing to land
- Prioritize reset when setups are unavailable

## Recovery tips

- Vary trampoline usage timing
- Recover with changing heights and routes
- Preserve jump when edgeguarders commit early

## Common beginner mistakes

- Charging fruit in unsafe positions
- Repeating one fruit route every stock
- Ignoring stage control while setting up

## Key moves

- Bonus Fruit: neutral control
- Hydrant: lane manipulation
- Back air: pressure and kill
- Nair: scramble option

## Out of shield options

- Nair out of shield close answer
- Up smash on unsafe anti-air windows
- Jump reset when pressure is safely spaced

## Basic combos

- Fruit hit into aerial follow-up
- Hydrant interaction into corner carry
- Grab starter into pressure reset

## Kill confirms

- Fruit confirm at ledge
- Back air ledge callout
- Hydrant trap punish

## Matchup considerations

- Against rushdown, value quick fruit choices
- Against zoners, use hydrant to break projectile rhythm
- Against heavies, farm trap damage

## Practical gameplay advice

- Pac-Man wins through prepared structure
- Keep fruit selection matchup-aware
- Do not force setups when pressured

## Training priorities

- Fruit route consistency
- Hydrant trap drills
- Recovery route mix practice
