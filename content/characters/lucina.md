## Character overview

Lucina is a fundamentals swordie built on reliable spacing, edgeguards, and low-variance decision-making.

## Difficulty level

Beginner-friendly

## Playstyle identity

Win with clean neutral spacing and consistent advantage, then close stocks through edgeguards and ledge control.

## Strengths

- Consistent disjoint coverage
- Strong edgeguard toolkit
- Low execution barrier for stable play

## Weaknesses

- Less explosive comeback routes
- Needs strong fundamentals to open defense
- Can struggle to force kills without reads

## Best beginner gameplan

- Control mid-range with safe sword swings
- Push hits into corner and ledge
- Take low-risk edgeguard opportunities

## Neutral strategy

- Stay just outside burst range and whiff punish
- Use falling fair/nair to pressure safely
- Avoid overcommitting in scramble range

## Advantage state

- Juggle with up air while keeping center
- Ledge trap jump and neutral getup reliably
- Edgeguard when recovery line is obvious

## Disadvantage state

- Mix landings with drift and delayed airspeed
- Avoid panic counter in disadvantage
- Use platform resets to avoid corner panic

## Recovery tips

- Vary dolphin slash timing to ledge
- Recover low and avoid telegraphed high routes
- Do not burn jump without edgeguard confirmation

## Common beginner mistakes

- Autopilot fair spacing with no adaptation
- Forcing kill swings too early
- Linear recoveries

## Key moves

- Fair: neutral staple
- Nair: pressure and anti-air
- Up air: juggle tool
- Back air: ledge kill option

## Out of shield options

- Up-B out of shield punish
- Nair out of shield in close pressure
- Shield grab versus unsafe crossups

## Basic combos

- Landing aerial into tilt follow-up
- Up throw into juggle pressure
- Corner poke into ledge trap sequence

## Kill confirms

- Back air near ledge
- Edgeguard fair calls
- Up-B punish out of shield

## Matchup considerations

- Against zoners, use disciplined walk-in spacing
- Against rushdown, anti-air with disjoint timing
- Against heavies, maximize edgeguard reliability

## Practical gameplay advice

- Lucina wins by being harder to punish
- Take stable options in tense moments
- Adapt ledge timing patterns each game

## Training priorities

- Aerial spacing consistency
- Edgeguard route practice
- OOS punish recognition
