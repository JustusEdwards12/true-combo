## Character overview

Yoshi is an aerial mobility bruiser with strong anti-juggle tools and explosive punish potential.

## Difficulty level

Beginner-friendly

## Playstyle identity

Pressure through drift-heavy aerials and armor jump utility while forcing opponents into anti-air mistakes.

## Strengths

- Excellent aerial drift and pressure
- Double jump armor helps disadvantage escapes
- Strong combo and kill options from close hits

## Weaknesses

- Can be punished for predictable landing aerials
- Recovery distance is limited without resources
- Grounded burst options are less threatening than air game

## Best beginner gameplan

- Start neutral with safe aerial spacing
- Use egg throw to disrupt approaches
- Capitalize on juggle and ledge pressure quickly

## Neutral strategy

- Drift around burst range and bait anti-air
- Mix empty landings with aerial pressure
- Avoid autopilot full-hop approaches

## Advantage state

- Convert anti-air hits into up air chains
- Force corner panic with repeated aerial threat
- Use ledge traps instead of risky spikes

## Disadvantage state

- Use armor jump thoughtfully, not instantly
- Vary landing timing with drift and fast-fall
- Do not panic airdodge to stage

## Recovery tips

- Mix double jump timing and angle
- Recover low to avoid pre-emptive anti-air
- Use egg stall only as occasional mix

## Common beginner mistakes

- Always using jump armor immediately
- Aerial mashing into shield
- Telegraphed recovery timing

## Key moves

- Nair: pressure and combo utility
- Up air: juggle finisher
- Egg Throw: space control
- Back air: kill pressure

## Out of shield options

- Nair out of shield for close responses
- Up smash on unsafe anti-air windows
- Jump reset when pressure is safe

## Basic combos

- Up tilt into up air chain
- Nair starter into tech chase
- Down throw into aerial pressure

## Kill confirms

- Back air at ledge
- Up air callout
- F-smash read after conditioning

## Matchup considerations

- Against zoners, use egg and drift to close safely
- Against swordies, avoid linear jump entries
- Against heavies, push advantage through juggle loops

## Practical gameplay advice

- Yoshi is strongest when movement is varied
- Do not overtrust armor as a panic answer
- Keep pressure safe before committing kills

## Training priorities

- Drift mixup repetition
- Armor jump decision drills
- Ledge pressure consistency
