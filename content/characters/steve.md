## Character overview

Steve is a resource-based trap character who controls tempo through block setups and explosive conversions.

## Difficulty level

Advanced

## Playstyle identity

Mine for upgrades safely, build structures that limit movement, then punish panic with high-reward confirms.

## Strengths

- Unique stage control with blocks
- High conversion reward when resources are strong
- Strong ledge traps and anti-approach tools

## Weaknesses

- Resource management adds mental load
- Can be overwhelmed if pressured during mining
- Recovery and escape routes can get read

## Best beginner gameplan

- Secure safe mining windows early
- Use blocks to control burst lanes
- Convert openings into corner traps and stocks

## Neutral strategy

- Alternate mining with movement so rhythm is not free
- Use blocks to force awkward approach angles
- Respect pressure characters who can break setup quickly

## Advantage state

- Trap ledge with block placements
- Use up tilt ladders when starter is clean
- Keep resource-aware pressure instead of random aggression

## Disadvantage state

- Do not panic minecart from bad ranges
- Mix landing and drift through block cover
- Protect jump for recovery ambiguity

## Recovery tips

- Vary elytra pathing and block assists
- Avoid repeating minecart recoveries
- Recover with resource awareness

## Common beginner mistakes

- Mining in unsafe positions
- Overreliance on minecart as panic button
- Ignoring material economy mid-set

## Key moves

- Blocks: stage control engine
- Minecart: burst and trap option
- Up tilt: combo starter
- Anvil: anti-air/landing punish

## Out of shield options

- Up smash out of shield on close pressure
- Nair out of shield in scrambles
- Jump reset when pressure spacing is safe

## Basic combos

- Up tilt starter into ladder routes
- Jab pressure into corner carry
- Block trap into aerial confirm

## Kill confirms

- Anvil landing punishes
- Minecart read at ledge
- Forward smash from block trap setup

## Matchup considerations

- Against rushdown, mine only behind structure
- Against zoners, use blocks to bypass projectile paths
- Against heavies, maximize trap damage

## Practical gameplay advice

- Steve is strongest with deliberate tempo
- Resource discipline decides long sets
- Adapt structure placement to matchup speed

## Training priorities

- Mining window recognition
- Block trap setup reps
- Resource-aware conversion practice
