## Character overview

Marth is a precision sword character whose tipper spacing transforms neutral wins into early stocks.

## Difficulty level

Intermediate

## Playstyle identity

Control exact spacing lanes, fish for tipper threat windows, and punish panic habits at ledge.

## Strengths

- High reward from precise spacing
- Strong edgeguard and ledgetrap threat
- Disjoint range controls neutral pace

## Weaknesses

- Inconsistent reward when spacing is sloppy
- Requires tight movement precision
- Can struggle in fast close-range scrambles

## Best beginner gameplan

- Play around tipper distance first
- Use safe pokes to force defensive drift
- Close stocks from corner tipper setups

## Neutral strategy

- Maintain exact burst range spacing
- Avoid rushing into untippered brawls
- Punish overshoots with retreating aerials

## Advantage state

- Use up air to juggle and reposition
- At ledge, hold tipper zone for jump or roll
- Reset to spacing control when sequence drops

## Disadvantage state

- Do not panic swing from juggle
- Use drift and timing mix to land
- Keep jump for recovery options

## Recovery tips

- Mix dolphin slash timing and angle
- Recover low when edgeguarders hold high
- Avoid repeated immediate ledge snaps

## Common beginner mistakes

- Ignoring tipper spacing goals
- Forcing close combat vs rushdown
- Overusing counter as panic

## Key moves

- Fair: spacing poke
- F-tilt: tipper threat
- Up air: juggle control
- Back air: ledge finisher

## Out of shield options

- Up-B out of shield punish
- Nair out of shield at close range
- Jump reset when pressure is too safe

## Basic combos

- Aerial poke into juggle pressure
- Down tilt into edge pressure
- Tipper poke into corner trap

## Kill confirms

- Tipper forward smash reads
- Back air at ledge
- Edgeguard fair callouts

## Matchup considerations

- Against zoners, prioritize spacing before approach
- Against rushdown, hold anti-air tipper lanes
- Against heavies, abuse ledge tipper pressure

## Practical gameplay advice

- Marth needs discipline and exactness
- Do not chase non-tipper scraps
- Let spacing create your kill windows

## Training priorities

- Tipper distance reps
- Ledge spacing drills
- Movement precision practice
