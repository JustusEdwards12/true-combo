## Character overview

Mewtwo is a floaty burst character with strong kill confirms and excellent mobility, balanced by fragile defense.

## Difficulty level

Intermediate

## Playstyle identity

Use movement and shadow ball pressure to force mistakes, then punish with high-reward confirms.

## Strengths

- Strong movement and drift mixups
- Shadow Ball controls neutral tempo
- Great kill threat from confirms and reads

## Weaknesses

- Large tail hurtbox in scrambles
- Light durability can lose stocks early
- Needs careful spacing in close combat

## Best beginner gameplan

- Charge shadow ball in safe windows
- Use disjointed aerial pressure at mid-range
- Close stocks by forcing panic in corner

## Neutral strategy

- Alternate charged and uncharged shadow ball timings
- Play around burst range and avoid raw commitments
- Use teleport movement to reset rhythm

## Advantage state

- Juggle with up air and teleport repositioning
- Ledge trap with shadow ball threat
- Push opponents into defensive panic

## Disadvantage state

- Do not panic teleport into predictable spots
- Mix landing drift and delayed aerial
- Keep jump available for recovery route changes

## Recovery tips

- Vary teleport angles and timing
- Recover low when edgeguard coverage is high
- Avoid repeated directional airdodge

## Common beginner mistakes

- Charging shadow ball too close to opponent
- Overcommitting kill attempts in neutral
- Predictable teleport recoveries

## Key moves

- Shadow Ball: neutral and kill threat
- Nair: pressure and combo utility
- Up air: juggle/kill
- Teleport: mobility and recovery

## Out of shield options

- Nair out of shield in scramble range
- Up smash on unsafe close anti-airs
- Jump reset against safe pressure

## Basic combos

- Down tilt or grab into aerial routes
- Shadow ball hit into pressure extension
- Nair starter into juggle state

## Kill confirms

- Shadow ball confirms at high percent
- Back air at ledge
- Up air callout in juggle

## Matchup considerations

- Against rushdown, rely on spacing and movement baits
- Against zoners, contest with shadow ball pacing
- Against heavies, maximize punish conversion

## Practical gameplay advice

- Mewtwo needs clean spacing discipline
- Keep neutral layered with movement and projectile
- Adapt teleport routes every stock

## Training priorities

- Shadow ball timing drills
- Teleport recovery mix practice
- Kill confirm consistency
