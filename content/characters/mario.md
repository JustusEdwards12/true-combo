Mario is the textbook **fundamentals character**: straightforward tools, honest disadvantage, and a skill curve that tracks your decision-making more than your tech skill.

## Strengths

- Flexible **aerial wall** that controls space without huge commitment.
- Converts stray hits into **damage** consistently at beginner-friendly percents.
- Strong **out-of-shield** game once you learn your ranges.

## Watchouts

- It is easy to **overcommit** to approaches that look “safe” online but get called out offline.
- Without disciplined spacing, Mario can feel outranged by true sword characters.
