## Character overview

Luigi is a close-range punish character who can swing stocks quickly once he gets in.

## Difficulty level

Intermediate

## Playstyle identity

Threaten grab and close-range frame traps, then convert into high-reward strings and ledge pressure.

## Strengths

- Explosive punish game from grab
- Strong kill throws and close confirms
- Reliable OOS threat when spaced for it

## Weaknesses

- Weak mid-range neutral without setup
- Struggles against strong disjoints
- Recovery gets targeted if route is obvious

## Best beginner gameplan

- Use fireball and movement feints to enter safely
- Condition shield, then threaten grab
- Close stocks through ledge and throw pressure

## Neutral strategy

- Play just outside burst range before dash-in attempts
- Do not force risky cyclones in neutral
- Use grounded patience to avoid getting whiff punished

## Advantage state

- Keep combo extensions controlled, not greedy
- At ledge, trap jump and neutral getup with short options
- Use throw positioning for stage control

## Disadvantage state

- Mix landing with drift and delayed aerials
- Avoid panic cyclone from unsafe heights
- Protect jump when being juggled

## Recovery tips

- Vary green missile timing and angle
- Use tornado only when route is clear
- Recover low and mix ledge snap timing

## Common beginner mistakes

- Fishing grab in obvious rhythm
- Overcommitting combo routes and dropping stage control
- Predictable recovery specials

## Key moves

- Grab: core win condition
- Nair: close scramble control
- Down tilt: grounded poke
- Back air: kill pressure

## Out of shield options

- Up-B out of shield as hard punish
- Nair out of shield for faster relief
- Shield grab against point-blank pressure

## Basic combos

- Grab starter into percent-heavy route
- Down throw into aerial extension
- Nair pressure into corner carry

## Kill confirms

- Back throw near ledge
- Up-B out of shield punish
- Back air whiff punish

## Matchup considerations

- Against swordies, pick safer approach lanes
- Against zoners, walk-shield patiently
- Against heavies, maximize grab punishes

## Practical gameplay advice

- Luigi needs disciplined entries
- Do not chase impossible confirms under pressure
- Adapt grab timing each game

## Training priorities

- Grab conversion consistency
- Recovery mix route drills
- Neutral entry practice versus disjoints
