## Character overview

R.O.B. is a spacing and setup character with strong gyro control, anti-air pressure, and reliable kill options.

## Difficulty level

Beginner-friendly

## Playstyle identity

Use gyro and laser to force movement, then capitalize with aerial pressure and ledge traps.

## Strengths

- Powerful neutral control with gyro
- Strong juggle and ledgetrap flow
- Reliable kill confirms from common situations

## Weaknesses

- Large hurtbox in disadvantage
- Can be rushed before setup
- Resource handling matters in scramble states

## Best beginner gameplan

- Set gyro safely and control center
- Use aerial pressure to force defensive mistakes
- Close stocks from ledge and anti-air traps

## Neutral strategy

- Alternate gyro toss timing to avoid predictable entries
- Use laser to interrupt long-range movement
- Stay aware of opponent burst range before item pull

## Advantage state

- Juggle with up air while holding stage
- At ledge, combine gyro and aerial coverage
- Trap panic options after item pressure

## Disadvantage state

- Do not panic side-B in juggles
- Mix landing drift and defensive timings
- Use platforms to reset pressure

## Recovery tips

- Vary fuel timing and side routes
- Recover low when anti-air edgeguards are waiting
- Avoid repeating same fuel burn pattern

## Common beginner mistakes

- Gyro pull in unsafe scramble
- Aerial mashing into shield
- Predictable fuel usage on recovery

## Key moves

- Gyro: neutral and trap engine
- Nair: pressure and combo starter
- Up air: juggle kill tool
- Laser: pacing control

## Out of shield options

- Nair out of shield in close pressure
- Up smash on obvious unsafe jumps
- Shield grab versus close overcommitments

## Basic combos

- Nair into aerial pressure
- Gyro hit into follow-up
- Down throw into anti-air route

## Kill confirms

- Up air juggle finish
- Back air at ledge
- Gyro trap into smash punish

## Matchup considerations

- Against rushdown, prioritize safe item timing
- Against zoners, contest pace with laser and gyro
- Against heavies, push ledge trap loops

## Practical gameplay advice

- R.O.B. thrives when setup and spacing are synced
- Do not abandon center for low-value chases
- Adapt gyro routes to opponent habits

## Training priorities

- Gyro confirm reps
- Fuel management recovery drills
- Ledge trap sequencing
