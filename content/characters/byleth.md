## Character overview

Byleth is a long-range disjoint character with heavy punish reward and potent ledge kill threat.

## Difficulty level

Intermediate

## Playstyle identity

Control neutral from range, force entries, and punish panic with high-damage confirms and edge traps.

## Strengths

- Huge disjoint spacing
- Strong kill power from confirms and reads
- Excellent ledge trap threat

## Weaknesses

- Can be overwhelmed in close-range speed matchups
- Slower buttons are punishable when whiffed
- Needs disciplined defensive decisions

## Best beginner gameplan

- Hold preferred range with safe pokes
- Punish overextensions with disjointed aerials
- Close stocks from corner traps

## Neutral strategy

- Stay outside burst range and force approach
- Do not overcommit to slow options
- Use movement feints before pressing

## Advantage state

- Juggle with up air and platform control
- At ledge, rotate between poke and anti-jump
- Use low-risk pressure to maintain lead

## Disadvantage state

- Avoid panic down-air or side special escapes
- Mix drift and landings to avoid anti-air scripts
- Reset with platform routes when possible

## Recovery tips

- Use tether creatively around edgeguard spacing
- Vary up-B timing
- Save jump to react to offstage pressure

## Common beginner mistakes

- Fishing hard-read smashes in neutral
- Ignoring burst range against fast characters
- Predictable tether timing

## Key moves

- Nair: spacing and combo glue
- Fair: long-range poke
- Up air: juggle finisher
- Down tilt: grounded check

## Out of shield options

- Nair out of shield in close pressure
- Up-B out of shield punish
- Shield grab when opponents overcommit

## Basic combos

- Nair into aerial follow-up
- Down tilt into pressure extension
- Aerial poke into ledge carry

## Kill confirms

- Back air corner punish
- Ledge trap callout with heavy swing
- Up air anti-air finish

## Matchup considerations

- Against rushdown, prioritize spacing over trades
- Against zoners, advance with shield discipline
- Against heavies, abuse edge pressure

## Practical gameplay advice

- Byleth thrives on patience and precision
- Take guaranteed punishes over flashy reads
- Adapt ledge timing coverage per opponent

## Training priorities

- Disjoint spacing reps
- Tether recovery mix
- Ledge trap consistency
