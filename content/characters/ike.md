## Character overview

Ike is a power sword character with threatening aerial range and reliable kill setups from strong reads.

## Difficulty level

Beginner-friendly

## Playstyle identity

Use large hitboxes to control airspace, then convert openings into juggle pressure and corner kills.

## Strengths

- Huge aerial hitboxes
- Strong kill threat from confirms and reads
- Simple gameplan with high reward

## Weaknesses

- Slower frame speed in close scrambles
- Can get outmaneuvered by quick rushdown
- Recovery can be intercepted

## Best beginner gameplan

- Establish nair and fair spacing in neutral
- Push hits into juggle and ledge situations
- Close with edge pressure and confirms

## Neutral strategy

- Play at sword tip and avoid panic swings
- Use empty movement to bait anti-air attempts
- Respect fast burst options before committing

## Advantage state

- Juggle with up air while staying centered
- At ledge, threaten jump and neutral getup
- Keep pressure simple and repeatable

## Disadvantage state

- Avoid panic airdodge to stage
- Mix drift and delayed landings
- Choose safe reset over risky reversal

## Recovery tips

- Vary up-B timing and side route
- Recover low when edgeguarders wait high
- Use jump late to avoid pre-reads

## Common beginner mistakes

- Autopilot nair from same spacing
- Overusing smash attacks in neutral
- Predictable up-B recovery timing

## Key moves

- Nair: neutral core
- Up air: juggle and kill threat
- Back air: corner kill
- F-tilt: grounded punish

## Out of shield options

- Nair out of shield in close pressure
- Up-B out of shield hard punish
- Shield grab on unsafe close hits

## Basic combos

- Nair starter into up air pressure
- Down throw into anti-air route
- Aerial hit into ledge carry

## Kill confirms

- Up air juggle finish
- Back air at ledge
- Nair confirm into kill route at high percent

## Matchup considerations

- Against zoners, walk in carefully and hold center
- Against rushdown, punish overextensions with big disjoints
- Against heavies, maximize edge pressure

## Practical gameplay advice

- Ike thrives on spacing confidence
- Take guaranteed pressure over risky reads
- Adapt nair timing when opponents parry

## Training priorities

- Nair spacing drill
- Juggle tracking reps
- Recovery route variation
