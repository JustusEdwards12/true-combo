## Character overview

Terry is a close-range strike character with powerful pressure strings and dangerous high-percent threat.

## Difficulty level

Intermediate

## Playstyle identity

Box your way into close range, pressure shield safely, and convert into high-damage confirms when openings appear.

## Strengths

- Strong grounded pressure options
- High reward from close confirms
- GO state adds comeback potential

## Weaknesses

- Can be kited by disjoints and zoning
- Recovery is exploitable if linear
- Needs spacing discipline to enter safely

## Best beginner gameplan

- Use crack shoot and pokes to enter range safely
- Condition shield with safe pressure
- Close stocks through confirms and ledge traps

## Neutral strategy

- Play just outside grab range before dashing in
- Avoid random burn knuckle in neutral
- Respect burst range of faster sword characters

## Advantage state

- Use pressure strings to hold corner
- Punish panic jump with anti-air specials
- With GO, threaten confirms but stay disciplined

## Disadvantage state

- Avoid panic power dunk in escape
- Mix drift and landings to reset neutral
- Use defensive patience when cornered

## Recovery tips

- Mix rising tackle and side special timing
- Recover low when anti-air coverage is obvious
- Save jump to avoid forced edgeguards

## Common beginner mistakes

- Mashing specials into shield
- Entering from same burst distance
- Overforcing GO confirms

## Key moves

- Jab: pressure starter
- Crack Shoot: burst entry
- Power Dunk: punish tool
- Back air: kill pressure

## Out of shield options

- Rising Tackle out of shield
- Jab out of shield in close scrambles
- Shield grab when pressure is too close

## Basic combos

- Jab confirms into specials
- Down tilt pressure into follow-up
- Aerial hit into corner carry

## Kill confirms

- GO confirms from grounded starters
- Back air at ledge
- Punish reads with up smash

## Matchup considerations

- Against zoners, walk-shield to enter safely
- Against swordies, punish overextensions hard
- Against heavies, pressure shield then command space

## Practical gameplay advice

- Terry excels when entry timing is controlled
- Keep confirms practical under pressure
- Adapt pressure timing every game

## Training priorities

- Jab confirm reliability
- Entry spacing drills
- Recovery mix route practice
