## Character overview

Isabelle is a defensive trap character who controls pace with slingshot pressure, fishing rod threat, and stage denial.

## Difficulty level

Intermediate

## Playstyle identity

Slow the game down, force predictable approaches, and punish panic habits with prepared traps.

## Strengths

- Strong trap and lane control
- Useful projectile harassment
- Good ledge pressure when setups are ready

## Weaknesses

- Can be overwhelmed by rushdown
- Close-range scrambles are risky
- Needs strong adaptation to secure kills

## Best beginner gameplan

- Set mine/trap positions early
- Use slingshot to control burst range
- Punish impatient entries with rod and anti-air

## Neutral strategy

- Alternate slingshot timing and movement
- Avoid pulling setups in unsafe range
- Hold center while forcing jump commitments

## Advantage state

- At ledge, layer trap plus projectile coverage
- Catch panic jumps with anti-air timing
- Use pressure resets to keep control

## Disadvantage state

- Do not panic rod as reversal
- Mix landing timing and drift
- Use patient shielding to reset pace

## Recovery tips

- Vary balloon recovery heights
- Recover wide to avoid direct edgeguard line
- Avoid repeating immediate ledge options

## Common beginner mistakes

- Trap setup in scramble distance
- Predictable slingshot rhythm
- Forcing kill reads without conditioning

## Key moves

- Slingshot: neutral chip
- Lloid Trap: stage denial
- Fishing Rod: punish and kill threat
- Nair: scramble response

## Out of shield options

- Nair out of shield in close pressure
- Grab out of shield vs unsafe pressure
- Jump reset when pressure is safe

## Basic combos

- Slingshot hit into positional advantage
- Trap trigger into aerial follow-up
- Grab starter into corner pressure

## Kill confirms

- Fishing rod callouts at ledge
- Trap plus anti-air confirms
- Back air corner punish

## Matchup considerations

- Against rushdown, prioritize defensive spacing
- Against zoners, use trap timing to disrupt rhythm
- Against heavies, farm ledge traps

## Practical gameplay advice

- Isabelle needs patience and preparation
- Let opponents run into your plans
- Adapt trap placement every game

## Training priorities

- Trap placement scenarios
- Rod punish timing practice
- Recovery height variation
