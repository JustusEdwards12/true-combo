## Character overview

Kazuya is a punish monster who thrives on close-range reads and devastating conversion routes.

## Difficulty level

Advanced

## Playstyle identity

Use disciplined spacing and shield pressure to get one opening, then convert it into massive damage or stocks.

## Strengths

- Extremely high punish reward
- Strong close-range defensive tools
- Can end stocks quickly from single reads

## Weaknesses

- Approach can be linear against spacing-heavy characters
- Weak mobility in extended neutral
- Recovery and disadvantage can be exploited

## Best beginner gameplan

- Hold grounded spacing and punish unsafe pressure
- Force close-range interactions with movement traps
- Prioritize guaranteed conversions over risky callouts

## Neutral strategy

- Use crouch dash movement to threaten burst range
- Do not chase opponents blindly across stage
- Respect projectiles and disjoints until you are in range

## Advantage state

- Convert starters into stable routes first
- At ledge, threaten both shield and jump panic
- Keep corner pressure rather than overextending offstage

## Disadvantage state

- Avoid panic attack from juggle states
- Mix drift and delayed defensive timing
- Preserve recovery resources for last-second route change

## Recovery tips

- Vary up-B timing and drift path
- Recover low to avoid obvious intercepts
- Avoid repeated air dodge to ledge

## Common beginner mistakes

- Fishing high-commit starters in neutral
- Dropping guaranteed confirms for style routes
- Predictable recovery timing

## Key moves

- Electric-style launcher: punish starter
- Jab pressure tools: close control
- Up-B: defensive punish
- Back air: spacing and kill

## Out of shield options

- Up-B out of shield punish
- Fast close option out of shield in scramble
- Shield hold reset when pressure is safe

## Basic combos

- Launcher starter into practical conversion
- Jab confirm into corner carry
- Grab starter into pressure reset

## Kill confirms

- Close-range launcher confirms
- Ledge trap punish routes
- Back air or smash read at corner

## Matchup considerations

- Against zoners, patient entry is mandatory
- Against swordies, punish overextension windows
- Against heavies, maximize one-touch damage

## Practical gameplay advice

- Kazuya rewards discipline over greed
- Stick to tournament-stable confirms
- Force respect, then convert hard

## Training priorities

- Core conversion consistency
- Entry timing against zoning
- Disadvantage survival drills
