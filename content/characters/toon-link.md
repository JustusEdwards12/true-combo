## Character overview

Toon Link is a floatier trap-based swordsman who excels at bomb setups and drift mixups around ledge.

## Difficulty level

Intermediate

## Playstyle identity

Use floaty movement and bomb control to create awkward timing windows, then punish with sword confirms.

## Strengths

- Strong bomb utility for ledge and neutral traps
- Floaty drift enables timing mixups
- Good edgeguard potential with aerial mobility

## Weaknesses

- Can get outpaced in close-range scrambles
- Kill setups require discipline and reads
- Landing can be called out if drift habits repeat

## Best beginner gameplan

- Establish bomb presence early
- Use zair and sword pokes to hold burst range
- Convert corner pressure with safe kill attempts

## Neutral strategy

- Alternate between projectile setup and empty repositioning
- Stay just outside dash-in burst options
- Avoid autopilot full-hop aerial entries

## Advantage state

- At ledge, rotate bomb throw timing
- Cover jump with rising fair or up air
- Trap landings with delayed swing rather than pre-swing

## Disadvantage state

- Drift unpredictably to platforms
- Do not always attack on the way down
- Protect double jump for last-second route changes

## Recovery tips

- Mix tether and spin recoveries
- Delay ledge snap to throw off edgeguard timing
- Use bomb drop defensively when safe

## Common beginner mistakes

- Using bomb pull in unsafe ranges
- Committing to slow smash reads in neutral
- Linear high recoveries under pressure

## Key moves

- Bomb: core trap and confirm tool
- Zair: spacing poke
- Back air: kill and ledge control
- Boomerang: neutral check

## Out of shield options

- Nair out of shield at scramble range
- Up-B as emergency punish
- Shield grab when pressure is too close

## Basic combos

- Bomb hit into aerial follow-up
- Down tilt into aerial at low percent
- Up throw into juggle pressure

## Kill confirms

- Bomb confirm into forward smash read
- Back air at ledge jump timing
- Up-B out of shield punish on unsafe pressure

## Matchup considerations

- Versus zoners, use bomb to contest projectile lanes
- Versus fast rushdown, hold safer mid-range
- Versus heavies, focus edgeguarding and corner traps

## Practical gameplay advice

- Toon Link wins off timing variety
- Play around bomb value, not random swings
- Keep stage control after each trap interaction

## Training priorities

- Bomb confirm consistency
- Ledge jump punish timing
- Drift and landing mixup reps
