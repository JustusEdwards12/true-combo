## Character overview

Donkey Kong is a heavyweight grappler with huge punish reward and strong corner intimidation.

## Difficulty level

Beginner-friendly

## Playstyle identity

Use threatening range and grab pressure to force defensive panic, then convert into juggle or ledge advantage.

## Strengths

- High damage per opening
- Excellent grab threat and cargo pressure
- Strong kill power without complicated execution

## Weaknesses

- Large hurtbox in disadvantage
- Can be juggled heavily by anti-air specialists
- Recovery can be intercepted if predictable

## Best beginner gameplan

- Control close-mid range with safe pokes
- Use grab threat to freeze shield
- Push opponents to ledge where heavy hits matter

## Neutral strategy

- Walk and shield to hold space without overcommitting
- Punish unsafe landings with tilts and grabs
- Avoid desperate dash attacks from too far

## Advantage state

- Use cargo throw routes for stage position
- Juggle with up air while tracking airdodge habits
- Keep ledge pressure simple and consistent

## Disadvantage state

- Do not mash aerials while being juggled
- Mix landing through platform routes
- Preserve jump for recovery flexibility

## Recovery tips

- Vary spin timing to ledge
- Recover low when anti-air traps are waiting
- Do not always airdodge to ledge

## Common beginner mistakes

- Forcing unsafe smashes in neutral
- Ignoring stage control after cargo throws
- Repeating same landing aerial

## Key moves

- Back air: spacing and pressure
- Cargo throw: positional control
- Up air: juggle tool
- Down tilt: grounded check

## Out of shield options

- Up-B out of shield as punish
- Grab out of shield against close pressure
- Jump reset when pressure is too safe

## Basic combos

- Cargo throw into juggle positioning
- Nair into grab pressure at low percent
- Down tilt into ledge trap setup

## Kill confirms

- Back air at ledge
- Cargo throw edge scenarios
- Raw up smash on hard reads

## Matchup considerations

- Against zoners, stay patient and claim ground slowly
- Against swordies, respect disjoint and punish landings
- Against lightweights, prioritize corner traps

## Practical gameplay advice

- DK wins through fear and position
- Take guaranteed damage over risky reads
- Adapt to anti-grab habits quickly

## Training priorities

- Cargo throw route rehearsal
- Ledge trap consistency
- Disadvantage survival drills
