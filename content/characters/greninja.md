## Character overview

Greninja is a slippery whiff-punish character with strong burst movement and corner conversion potential.

## Difficulty level

Intermediate

## Playstyle identity

Bait swings with dash speed, punish recovery windows, and convert into edge pressure.

## Strengths

- Excellent movement and burst spacing
- Strong whiff punish toolkit
- Great corner carry and edge pressure

## Weaknesses

- Can struggle in prolonged shield pressure situations
- Some confirms require clean spacing
- Light weight punishes mistakes hard

## Best beginner gameplan

- Control neutral with movement feints
- Punish overextensions instead of forcing entries
- Turn openings into ledge pressure quickly

## Neutral strategy

- Play around opponent burst range with dash movement
- Use water shuriken to force responses, not autopilot
- Avoid predictable jump-in timings

## Advantage state

- Juggle with up air while tracking drift
- At ledge, threaten jump and roll with speed
- Keep pressure fluid but low-risk

## Disadvantage state

- Mix landings and fast-fall timings
- Avoid panic shadow sneak from pressure
- Use mobility to reset neutral

## Recovery tips

- Vary hydro pump angle and timing
- Recover low when anti-air traps are waiting
- Avoid repeated directional air dodge

## Common beginner mistakes

- Forcing dash attack from unsafe ranges
- Overusing shadow sneak as gimmick
- Predictable hydro pump routes

## Key moves

- Dash attack: burst starter
- Nair: neutral and combo glue
- Up air: juggle finisher
- Water shuriken: tempo control

## Out of shield options

- Nair out of shield in close scrambles
- Grab out of shield on unsafe pressure
- Jump reset against safe spacing

## Basic combos

- Dash attack starter into aerial routes
- Down tilt into follow-up pressure
- Nair chains into corner carry

## Kill confirms

- Back air ledge pressure
- Up smash anti-air punish
- Edgeguard callouts from speed advantage

## Matchup considerations

- Against zoners, close safely with movement
- Against swordies, rely on whiff punish windows
- Against heavies, maximize corner carry

## Practical gameplay advice

- Greninja wins by making opponents miss
- Do not trade hits unnecessarily
- Adapt burst timing every stock

## Training priorities

- Whiff punish reaction drills
- Hydro pump recovery routes
- Corner carry consistency
