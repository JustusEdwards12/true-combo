## Character overview

Wolf is a balanced pressure character with strong burst options, solid zoning tools, and reliable kill confirms.

## Difficulty level

Beginner-friendly

## Playstyle identity

Use blaster and movement to force impatience, then punish with aerials and grounded confirms.

## Strengths

- Strong neutral versatility
- Excellent kill power from common starters
- Reliable ledgetrap pressure

## Weaknesses

- Can become predictable if blaster cadence repeats
- Recovery is exploitable if linear
- Unsafe aerial spacing gets punished hard

## Best beginner gameplan

- Start neutral with blaster checks and safe pokes
- Use burst movement to punish defensive habits
- Close stocks through ledge pressure and confirms

## Neutral strategy

- Use blaster to force jump and shield responses
- Hold burst range for dash-in punishes
- Avoid autopilot full-hop aerials

## Advantage state

- Juggle with up air and shark platforms
- At ledge, trap jump with back air
- Use safe pressure resets instead of hard calls

## Disadvantage state

- Mix drift and fast-fall timing to land
- Do not always directional airdodge inward
- Reset through platform if corner pressure is heavy

## Recovery tips

- Vary side-B and up-B timing
- Recover low when possible
- Avoid same-distance side-B habits

## Common beginner mistakes

- Blaster spam from unsafe range
- Unsafe dash attack fishing
- Predictable side-B recoveries

## Key moves

- Blaster: neutral conditioning
- Nair: pressure starter
- Back air: kill pressure
- Dash attack: burst punish

## Out of shield options

- Nair out of shield
- Up smash on obvious unsafe landings
- Jump reset against safe pressure

## Basic combos

- Nair into dash attack or tilt
- Down throw into pressure follow-up
- Aerial hit into ledge carry

## Kill confirms

- Back air near ledge
- Up smash anti-air punish
- Dash attack edge pressure confirms

## Matchup considerations

- Against zoners, blaster to contest pace then advance
- Against swordies, play whiff punish lanes
- Against heavies, pressure safely and trap ledge

## Practical gameplay advice

- Wolf excels when tempo stays varied
- Use blaster with purpose, not habit
- Adapt kill routes to defensive choices

## Training priorities

- Blaster-to-entry timing reps
- Back air spacing drills
- Recovery route mix practice
