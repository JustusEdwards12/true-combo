## Character overview

Meta Knight is an aerial control character with strong edgeguard flow and burst combo potential from close starters.

## Difficulty level

Intermediate

## Playstyle identity

Win close interactions with quick aerials, then carry opponents into juggle and offstage pressure.

## Strengths

- Excellent aerial mobility
- Strong edgeguard and juggle capability
- Can convert clean starters into major momentum

## Weaknesses

- Short range in grounded neutral
- Needs precision to secure consistent kills
- Can be outranged by swords and zoners

## Best beginner gameplan

- Use movement and safe aerial entries to open neutral
- Push hits vertically and offstage
- Close stocks with edge pressure and anti-air reads

## Neutral strategy

- Play near burst range and whiff punish
- Do not autopilot dash attack entries
- Use multiple jump heights to vary approach

## Advantage state

- Juggle with up air while tracking DI
- At ledge, force jump panic and punish
- Use edgeguard routes that preserve stage

## Disadvantage state

- Avoid panic dimensional cape as escape
- Mix landing routes and timing
- Preserve jump count for recovery flexibility

## Recovery tips

- Use multi-jump variation before up-B
- Recover from different heights and drifts
- Avoid predictable ledge re-entry habits

## Common beginner mistakes

- Forcing unsafe specials in neutral
- Overchasing offstage without resource plan
- Landing with same aerial timing

## Key moves

- Nair: close pressure
- Up air: juggle core
- Dash attack: burst starter
- Back air: edgeguard pressure

## Out of shield options

- Nair out of shield in close pressure
- Up-B out of shield punish
- Jump reset when pressure spacing is safe

## Basic combos

- Dash attack starter into juggle route
- Nair into aerial extension
- Ledge poke into edgeguard continuation

## Kill confirms

- Up air string finisher at high percent
- Back air offstage callout
- Ledge trap read into smash

## Matchup considerations

- Against zoners, close distance with patient jumps
- Against swordies, play around startup not hitbox end
- Against heavies, abuse edgeguard opportunities

## Practical gameplay advice

- Meta Knight wins through movement precision
- Do not force deep edgeguards when ahead
- Adapt jump timing frequently

## Training priorities

- Aerial movement drills
- Edgeguard route rehearsal
- Juggle DI tracking reps
