## Character overview

Sephiroth is a long-range sword specialist who controls space with precise spacing and punishes overextensions brutally.

## Difficulty level

Intermediate

## Playstyle identity

Dominate neutral at range, force unsafe entries, then convert with wing-enhanced pressure and ledge traps.

## Strengths

- Exceptional disjoint range
- Strong ledge and edgeguard presence
- Wing state boosts comeback pressure

## Weaknesses

- Light and vulnerable in close scrambles
- Can be rushed if spacing discipline slips
- Unsafe commitments are heavily punished

## Best beginner gameplan

- Establish long-range control first
- Punish impatience with spaced aerials and tilts
- Use wing state to shift tempo when active

## Neutral strategy

- Stay at tipper spacing and avoid brawling range
- Use shadow flare to influence movement
- Respect burst options from fast characters

## Advantage state

- Juggle with up air from below
- At ledge, rotate between long-range poke and grab bait
- Use pressure resets to maintain control

## Disadvantage state

- Avoid panic aerial swings at point-blank range
- Drift to platform and reset when cornered
- Protect jump to avoid forced edgeguards

## Recovery tips

- Mix octaslash timing and route
- Use side-B pressure to deter hard chases
- Recover from varied heights

## Common beginner mistakes

- Overcommitting to slow kill swings
- Letting opponents into close range for free
- Predictable recovery angles

## Key moves

- F-tilt: spacing wall
- Back air: kill and ledge control
- Shadow Flare: conditioning tool
- Up air: juggle finisher

## Out of shield options

- Nair out of shield in tight pressure
- Up-B out of shield when opponents overcommit
- Jump reset if pressure is well spaced

## Basic combos

- Tilt poke into aerial follow-up
- Shadow flare setup into corner pressure
- Nair starter into juggle state

## Kill confirms

- Back air at ledge
- F-smash hard read in corner
- Edgeguard aerial callouts

## Matchup considerations

- Against rushdown, hold strict spacing lines
- Against zoners, use sword range to bypass projectile rhythm
- Against heavies, abuse disjoint and ledge control

## Practical gameplay advice

- Sephiroth wins through precision and patience
- Do not force close-range scrambles
- Adapt spacing lane every game

## Training priorities

- Tipper spacing drills
- Ledge trap coverage practice
- Recovery route mixups
