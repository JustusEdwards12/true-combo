## Character overview

Ganondorf is a hard-read powerhouse with devastating punishes but limited defensive mobility.

## Difficulty level

Beginner-friendly

## Playstyle identity

Threaten massive punish damage through spacing traps and corner reads while minimizing disadvantage exposure.

## Strengths

- Huge kill power from simple openings
- Strong intimidation factor in close-mid range
- Can end stocks early with good reads

## Weaknesses

- Severe disadvantage and recovery weaknesses
- Slow startup can be exploited
- Gets kited by mobility and projectiles

## Best beginner gameplan

- Hold center and punish overextensions
- Use safe pokes before committing heavy swings
- Convert one read into stock advantage

## Neutral strategy

- Stay just outside opponent burst range
- Use dash back traps to bait panic entries
- Avoid gambling with raw smash attacks

## Advantage state

- Ledge trap with broad hitboxes
- Punish panic jumps with up air and fair
- Keep pressure simple and guaranteed

## Disadvantage state

- Prioritize survival over reversal attempts
- Mix drift and delayed landing
- Do not burn jump too early

## Recovery tips

- Recover low with varied timing
- Use side-B sparingly as mixup
- Avoid obvious up-B routes

## Common beginner mistakes

- Swinging constantly in neutral
- Ignoring stage control for risky edgeguards
- Repeat recovery timing

## Key moves

- Nair: neutral check
- Down tilt: spacing punish
- Back air: kill option
- Flame Choke: habit trap

## Out of shield options

- Nair out of shield in close pressure
- Up-B out of shield hard punish
- Shield grab when pressure is unsafe

## Basic combos

- Flame Choke tech-chase pressure
- Nair into corner follow-up
- Down throw into anti-air read

## Kill confirms

- Back air ledge punishes
- F-tilt and smash callouts
- Flame Choke read into finisher

## Matchup considerations

- Against zoners, be patient and claim ground
- Against rushdown, punish overcommitment hard
- Against heavies, keep corner dominance

## Practical gameplay advice

- Ganondorf needs disciplined risk management
- Let opponents make the first big mistake
- Value each stock lead heavily

## Training priorities

- Tech-chase read practice
- Ledge trap consistency
- Recovery survival drills
