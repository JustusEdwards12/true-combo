## Character overview

Palutena is a balanced control character with strong aerial pressure, anti-air tools, and stable ledge flow.

## Difficulty level

Beginner-friendly

## Playstyle identity

Use safe aerial spacing and movement control to dominate neutral, then convert into juggle and ledge pressure.

## Strengths

- Excellent safe neutral tools
- Reliable juggle and ledgetrap game
- Strong consistency in long sets

## Weaknesses

- Can be predictable if nair cadence repeats
- Relies on adaptation for kill confirms
- Some options are punishable when overused

## Best beginner gameplan

- Build neutral with safe aerials and dash spacing
- Push opponents to corner before forcing kills
- Convert anti-air wins into stock pressure

## Neutral strategy

- Use retreating aerials to test reactions
- Avoid autopilot dash attack entries
- Play around burst range and whiff punish windows

## Advantage state

- Shark landings with up air positioning
- At ledge, cover jump and neutral getup reliably
- Maintain center after each exchange

## Disadvantage state

- Mix drift and delayed falls to land
- Do not panic teleport from unsafe spots
- Save jump for clear escape route

## Recovery tips

- Vary teleport timing and angle
- Recover low when edgeguard commitment appears high
- Avoid repeating immediate ledge options

## Common beginner mistakes

- Overusing nair with same timing
- Forcing explosive flame in bad spots
- Linear teleport recoveries

## Key moves

- Nair: pressure and combo starter
- Back air: kill/spacing tool
- Up air: juggle control
- Explosive Flame: space trap

## Out of shield options

- Nair out of shield
- Grab out of shield against close pressure
- Jump reset against safe spacing

## Basic combos

- Nair chains into corner carry
- Down throw into aerial pressure
- Dash attack punish into ledge state

## Kill confirms

- Back air at ledge
- Up air anti-air finish
- Read-based forward smash in corner

## Matchup considerations

- Against zoners, close distance with safe movement
- Against rushdown, anti-air consistently
- Against heavies, loop ledge pressure

## Practical gameplay advice

- Palutena excels through consistency
- Rotate pressure timing to avoid parry traps
- Keep stage control as primary goal

## Training priorities

- Nair spacing and drift
- Teleport recovery mix
- Ledge trap route rehearsal
