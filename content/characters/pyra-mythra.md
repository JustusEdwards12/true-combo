## Character overview

Pyra and Mythra are a stance-shift duo: Mythra wins neutral and tempo, while Pyra secures stocks and threat value.

## Difficulty level

Intermediate

## Playstyle identity

Use Mythra to build control and advantage, then switch to Pyra for ledge pressure and kill conversions.

## Strengths

- Excellent neutral speed with Mythra
- Strong kill power and ledge threat with Pyra
- Flexible adaptation through character switching

## Weaknesses

- Predictable switches can be punished
- Pyra can be whiff punished if overused in neutral
- Mythra may struggle to finish stocks without setup

## Best beginner gameplan

- Start neutral with Mythra movement and safe pokes
- Build percent before forcing kill attempts
- Swap to Pyra when corner pressure is established

## Neutral strategy

- Use Mythra to contest burst range and whiffs
- Do not autopilot Pyra entries in neutral
- Condition shield before swap-based pressure

## Advantage state

- Mythra juggles into corner carry
- Pyra threatens ledge with high-reward swings
- Switch based on state, not habit

## Disadvantage state

- Use Mythra mobility to escape pressure
- Avoid panic switch under heavy pressure
- Preserve jump for safe landings

## Recovery tips

- Mix both recoveries based on edgeguard position
- Recover low when opponents pre-aim high
- Avoid repeated side special routes

## Common beginner mistakes

- Staying Pyra too long in fast neutral
- Switching predictably after each hit
- Forcing Mythra kill options without setup

## Key moves

- Mythra nair/fair: neutral staples
- Mythra up air: juggle pressure
- Pyra back air: kill pressure
- Pyra up smash: anti-air punish

## Out of shield options

- Mythra quick aerial out of shield
- Pyra up-B out of shield for hard punish
- Shield grab when opponents overextend

## Basic combos

- Mythra tilt into aerial strings
- Mythra starter into corner carry
- Pyra hit into ledge pressure setup

## Kill confirms

- Pyra back air at ledge
- Pyra smash punish on panic landings
- Edgeguard callout with Pyra aerials

## Matchup considerations

- Against rushdown, rely on Mythra speed
- Against heavies, rotate Mythra damage then Pyra finishers
- Against zoners, use Mythra movement to claim space

## Practical gameplay advice

- Treat switching as strategy, not reflex
- Keep center before committing to Pyra swings
- Adapt forms to opponent habits

## Training priorities

- Swap timing decision drills
- Mythra conversion consistency
- Pyra ledge trap routes
