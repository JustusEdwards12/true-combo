This matchup is a **range negotiation**: Cloud wants to pin Mario to the end of his sword; Mario wants to slip into **grab and aerial** windows without eating disjoints.

## What Cloud is trying to do

- **Wall you out** with mid-range pokes until you jump or shield predictably.
- Convert small hits into **ledge scenarios** where his traps are streamlined.

## Mario adjustments

- Approach in **layers**: threaten empty movement, then commit when Cloud’s option is locked.
- Prefer **cross-ups** and tight drift over linear full hops that lose to big aerials.
- When Cloud has **limit**, simplify—take space, reset, and force a whiff instead of trading blind.
