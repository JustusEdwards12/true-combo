## Intro

Whiff punishing means baiting a move, letting it miss, and striking the recovery window. It is one of the cleanest ways to win neutral consistently.

## Practical Examples

- Dash back from Roy short-hop fair, then whiff punish landing lag.
- Shield a minus aerial at max range, then take stage instead of forcing punish.
- Empty hop bait into grounded anti-air when opponent swings early.

## Common Mistakes

- Pre-committing punish before move actually whiffs.
- Trying to whiff punish without first controlling burst range.
- Using slow punish options that lose to recovery movement.

## Focus First

Choose one bait pattern and one punish option for your main, then loop it in training mode against recorded CPU movement.

## In-Match Adjustments

- If opponent stops swinging, transition to pressure and grabs.
- If they mash spot-dodge after whiff, delay punish timing.
- If your punish misses, simplify to faster options.

## Quick Tips

- Patience is the core whiff punish skill.
- You only need one clean punish to retake tempo.
- Spacing setup matters more than reaction bragging.
