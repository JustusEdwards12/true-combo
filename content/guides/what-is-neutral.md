**Neutral** is the phase where neither player has a guaranteed follow-up—both are jockeying for an opening. It is not “nothing is happening.” It is **risk management** disguised as a walk animation.

## The three exchange states

- **Neutral** — Players contest space and threaten options without a confirmed combo or locked situation.
- **Advantage** — One player has a positional or frame lead that forces a defensive response (shield, jump, escape).
- **Disadvantage** — One player is defending—off the ledge, in the air without jumps, or in tech situations.

## A practical way to review VODs

1. Mark the **first hit** of each exchange.
2. Ask what **habit** created the opening (spacing, drift, drift after aerial, land lag).
3. Write one **counter-adjustment** you can test next session.
