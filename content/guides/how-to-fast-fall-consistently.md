## Intro

Fast falling is the timing layer that makes your jump game threatening instead of floaty and punishable.

## Practical Examples

- Practice short hop aerial into delayed fast fall to hit shield at safer timings.
- Use empty jump fast fall to bait anti-air swings, then whiff punish.
- In juggle escape, vary fast-fall timing to avoid autopilot anti-air tracks.

## Common Mistakes

- Fast falling every jump at the same rhythm.
- Holding down too early and missing aerial spacing.
- Fast falling into corner pressure with no defensive plan.

## Focus First

Train three jump heights with different fast-fall timings before adding attacks.

## In-Match Adjustments

- If getting anti-aired, delay fast fall and drift out.
- If overshooting shield, fast fall earlier and fade back.
- If landing trapped, stop forcing aerial and empty land shield.

## Quick Tips

- Fast fall is a mixup tool, not a default.
- Pair with spacing references on stage.
- Review landings that lose stocks in VODs.
