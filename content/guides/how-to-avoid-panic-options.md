## Intro

Panic options happen when your mental stack collapses. The fix is not 'be calm'; it is building simple fallback decisions before pressure starts.

## Practical Examples

- At high percent, pre-plan one escape route from ledge and one from corner.
- After missed tech, choose hold shield once instead of instant roll.
- In juggle, save jump unless anti-air is already committed.

## Common Mistakes

- Auto-rolling after every blocked hit.
- Directional airdodge with no visual confirm.
- Throwing unsafe kill move when behind in tempo.

## Focus First

Create pressure scripts for three states: ledge, corner, and juggle. Repeat until they feel automatic.

## In-Match Adjustments

- If panic starts, slow your inputs for one interaction.
- If opponent farms one panic option, remove it for a full stock.
- If nerves spike, return to center-control goals.

## Quick Tips

- Preplanned defense beats emotional defense.
- One clean reset can change momentum.
- Composure is trainable with repetition.
