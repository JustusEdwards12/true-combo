## Intro

Safe landing is about unpredictability plus spacing. Strong defenders mix timing, drift, and resources instead of always mashing aerial or airdodge.

## Practical Examples

- Drift past opponent then fast-fall to platform for reset.
- Empty land shield once to scout anti-air habits.
- Use delayed aerial only when it threatens space, not by default.

## Common Mistakes

- Burning double jump too early in juggle.
- Directional airdodge to stage on repeat.
- Landing with same aerial timing every stock.

## Focus First

Practice three landing options in a loop: drift out, land shield, delayed aerial. Rotate without repeating pattern.

## In-Match Adjustments

- If juggled hard, favor platform escape before center challenge.
- If opponent waits for airdodge, keep resources longer.
- If cornered on landing, prioritize safe stage reclaim.

## Quick Tips

- Good landings preserve resources.
- Landing mixups start from jump path.
- Sometimes no button is best button.
