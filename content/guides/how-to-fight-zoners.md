## Intro

Beating zoners is about controlled approach layers. You win by shrinking their safe space and forcing close-range decisions on your terms.

## Practical Examples

- Walk-shield forward to reduce runaway room instead of full-hop from far away.
- Parry predictable projectile rhythm and take center stage first.
- Corner zoners and hold anti-jump pressure rather than chasing deep.

## Common Mistakes

- Approaching in straight line with same jump timing.
- Rushing from full screen and eating anti-approach tools.
- Overcommitting once inside and resetting spacing for free.

## Focus First

Train a three-step anti-zoner plan: safe entry, mid-range hold, and corner conversion.

## In-Match Adjustments

- If projectile tempo is fast, slow the game and walk-shield.
- If they jump back often, claim stage and anti-air landing.
- If they panic up close, keep pressure tight and simple.

## Quick Tips

- Patience beats frustration against zoners.
- Center stage is the first win condition.
- Use conditioning to open their shield.
